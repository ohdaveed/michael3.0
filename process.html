Our Process

Your Path to Peace of Mind

Six clear steps—from first call to funded trust—so you know what happens next at each stage.

Estate planning process steps

01

Free Initial Consultation

Duration: 45-60 minutes | Location: Our office, your home, or remote

Michael reviews your family, assets, and goals, then outlines the documents you need and quotes fees upfront—no pressure.

Send a message →
02

Design Your Plan

Duration: 1-2 weeks | Format: Detailed questionnaire

You complete a detailed questionnaire; we review answers together and lock the design before drafting begins.

03

Draft & Review Documents

Duration: 2-3 weeks | Revisions: Unlimited

Drafts arrive for your review. Ask anything—we revise until the language matches your intent and you understand each section.

04

Signing Ceremony

Duration: 60-90 minutes | Location: Our office or your home

Guided signing with witnesses and notary—spouses, trustees, or other signers coordinated in one sitting when possible.

05

Implementation & Asset Transfers

Duration: Ongoing support | Guidance: Step-by-step

Funding checklists for deeds, banks, and brokerage accounts; we prepare deeds and coach you through the rest so the trust actually holds assets.

06

Lifetime Support

Updates: As needed | Availability: Ongoing support

Email or use the contact form after births, marriages, moves, or big purchases—Michael amends plans as life evolves and later advises trustees or executors.
