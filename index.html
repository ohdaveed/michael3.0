Probate vs. planning (cost snapshot)

California sets minimum probate fees for the attorney and executor based on gross estate value:

$184,500 estate $5,980 minimum
$500,000 estate $13,000 minimum
$1,000,000 estate $23,000 minimum

Court costs, appraisals, and months of delay are extra. A funded Living Trust can skip this path for many families.
