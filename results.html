Case Study 01

Avoiding $47,000 in Probate Fees

The Challenge

San Francisco couple, late 60s, with a $1.8M home plus retirement and rentals. No plan meant ~$47k in statutory probate fees and 12–18+ months in court.

The Solution

Living Trust package, funded assets, named successor trustees, and added incapacity documents (POA + healthcare).

The Result

First death: assets flowed to survivor with no probate filing. Second death routes to kids via trust—avoiding repeat fees and public filings. Net effect: tens of thousands saved versus statutory probate.

Case Study 02

Protecting a Blended Family

The Challenge

Widower remarried; three adult kids from prior marriage. Needed spouse support for life without disinheriting children—high dispute risk without clear documents.

The Solution

Irrevocable trust structure: lifetime income/housing for spouse, remainder to children, with explicit instructions to reduce ambiguity.

The Result

Both sides signed off with shared understanding—spouse secure, children confident, family friction minimized.

Case Study 03

Streamlining Complex Trust Administration

The Challenge

Three siblings as equal beneficiaries; trust held real estate, a business, and investments. Eldest trustee overwhelmed by notices, taxes, and beneficiary updates.

The Solution

We inventoried assets, ran accounting, coordinated tax pros, supervised sales, and kept beneficiaries informed to avoid misunderstandings.

The Result

Closed in about eight months, California rules satisfied, ~$2.4M distributed without litigation, trustee shielded from DIY missteps.
