How We've Helped Families Protect What Matters

Every family's situation is unique. Here are examples of how comprehensive estate planning made a difference for our clients.

Case Study 01

Avoiding $47,000 in Probate Fees

The Challenge

A San Francisco couple in their late 60s owned a home worth $1.8M, retirement accounts, and investment properties. Without proper planning, their estate would face minimum probate fees of $47,000, plus court costs and delays of 12-18 months.

The Solution

We created a comprehensive Living Trust package that transferred all assets into the trust, appointed successor trustees, and established a clear distribution plan. We also implemented healthcare directives and powers of attorney to protect them if either became incapacitated.

The Result

Upon the first spouse's passing, assets transferred seamlessly to the surviving spouse with zero probate costs and no court involvement. When the second spouse passes, their children will inherit directly through the trust — again avoiding all probate fees and delays. Total savings: $47,000+ and 18 months of stress.

Case Study 02

Protecting a Blended Family

The Challenge

A widower with three adult children from his first marriage remarried. He wanted to ensure his new spouse would be cared for during her lifetime, while ultimately preserving his estate for his children. Without proper planning, potential disputes could have torn the family apart.

The Solution

We drafted an irrevocable trust that provides lifetime income and housing for his spouse, with the principal ultimately distributed to his children. The trust included specific provisions addressing both families' concerns and clear instructions that eliminated ambiguity.

The Result

All parties understood and agreed to the arrangement. The spouse has security and peace of mind, the children know their inheritance is protected, and the family maintains harmony. The detailed trust provisions prevent costly disputes and preserve family relationships.

Case Study 03

Streamlining Complex Trust Administration

The Challenge

After their father passed, three siblings inherited equal shares of a family trust containing real estate, business interests, and investment accounts. The trustee (eldest sibling) was overwhelmed by the legal obligations, tax requirements, and beneficiary communications required by law.

The Solution

We provided comprehensive trust administration services: inventoried and valued all assets, prepared accountings, filed necessary tax returns, facilitated property sales, and handled all legal compliance requirements. We also mediated communications between beneficiaries to prevent misunderstandings.

The Result

The trust was administered efficiently and in full compliance with California law. All assets were properly valued and distributed within 8 months. The siblings maintained their relationship, and the trustee was protected from potential liability. Final distributions totaled $2.4M with zero disputes.
